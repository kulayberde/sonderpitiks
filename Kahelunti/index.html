About Me

I'm Kris

a photographer and storyteller driven by the beauty of fleeting moments. Sonder, the realization that every person you pass has a life as vivid and complex as your own, is what fuels my work. Every image I take is a quiet testament to the emotions we often overlook—joy, solitude, nostalgia, and wonder. Through my lens, I hope to remind you: you are part of someone’s story, and someone is part of yours.
